Pac-Man Tribute is a lovingly crafted homage to the classic arcade game Pac-Man, released in 1980. Our team of passionate developers has worked tirelessly to recreate the nostalgic experience of chasing ghosts and munching on pellets.

Main Contributions of Each Team Member

  • Sergi Santiago Miguel: Lead Game Designer and Programmer.
  • Bruno Gomez: Art Director and Animator.
